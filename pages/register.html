You should create an account. Why? Just look at all these great sample pages showing how the user interface for the site works!

Your account allows you to create profile pages. You should pick a good name, because it will also be the name associated with your first profile. Names may be any mix of lowercase letters and numbers, no shorter than four characters.

Your password should be carefully selected. It must be at least six characters long, but there are no other restrictions. Don't lose your password, as there is currently no automatic recovery system!
